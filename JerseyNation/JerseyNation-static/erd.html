ERD Diagram

This page presents the ERD Diagram as an interactive image and the related Data Dictionary. Even for a relatively small website, many tables are needed to support proper data control, operational consistency, and secure administration. For that reason, the ERD was organized in business blocks. The top diagram provides the high-level interactive image, while the details of each block are presented below together with the Data Dictionary.

Business-block ERD View

The ERD is presented as an interactive image organized by business blocks. This structure improves readability and makes it easier to understand the relationships among domains such as customer, order, catalog, shipping, payment, supplier, administration, and marketing. The detailed description of each block and its related Data Dictionary are presented below.
